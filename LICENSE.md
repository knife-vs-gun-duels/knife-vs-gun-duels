## 🐛 Common Issues and Fixes

**Script won't execute**
Ensure your executor is fully attached to the Roblox process before running. Restart both the executor and Roblox, then try again.

**GUI doesn't appear after execution**
Press `RightShift` to toggle the dashboard. Some executors introduce a 2–4 second delay before the panel renders after execution.

**Hitbox expander not registering hits**
Lower the hitbox multiplier in GUI settings. Very high values can cause server-side rejection of hit events — a value between 3–6x is most reliable.

**Aimbot snapping unnaturally**
Increase the smoothness value in the aimbot settings tab. A smoothness of 0.3–0.5 produces the most human-like tracking behavior.

**Silent aim missing shots**
Silent aim works best at close-to-medium range. At very long distances, server-side lag compensation can reduce its effectiveness — close the gap before engaging.

**ESP flickering or disappearing mid-match**
This typically occurs after a server-side player sync event. Re-execute the script once to refresh the ESP overlay.

**Speed modifier causing rubber-banding**
Reduce the speed multiplier to 1.3–1.5x. Higher values trigger Roblox's built-in velocity sanity checks, causing rubber-band rollbacks.
